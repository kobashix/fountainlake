The Fountain Lake Network is a local-first news and information site created to serve the Fountain Lake, Arkansas community and surrounding areas.

Our goal is simple: provide clear, reliable updates on what matters locally — schools, events, public safety, businesses, and community announcements — without noise, algorithms, or clickbait.

Why This Exists

Small communities are often underrepresented or misrepresented by regional and national outlets. Important local information gets buried, delayed, or ignored entirely.

Fountain Lake Network exists to fill that gap with timely, straightforward coverage that respects readers’ time and intelligence.

What We Cover

Coverage includes local news, school updates, events, business activity, and site announcements. Content may come from original reporting, public information, and verified community submissions.

Independence & Transparency

This site is independently operated. There are no political affiliations, no sponsored narratives, and no engagement-driven sensationalism.

If something is corrected or updated, it will be done clearly and openly.
