Fountain Lake Arkansas Network

Local news, school updates, events, and business listings for Fountain Lake, Arkansas, including communities near Hot Springs National Park and Hot Springs Village.

The Fountain Lake Arkansas Network is a community-focused information hub serving residents and families in the Fountain Lake area. The community lies between Hot Springs National Park and Hot Springs Village, making it uniquely connected to both the city of Hot Springs and surrounding Garland County communities.
